Wanderkind Embassy

Every Way begins at your door.

Wanderkind installs as a small app on your home screen. No app store. No tracking. Just a door — yours, and the doors that open for you in return.

Install in two minutes

  1. Open this page in Safari. Other browsers can't install PWAs on iOS.
  2. Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari (the square with the upward arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Confirm. The Wanderkind seal appears on your home screen. Open it from there — it runs as its own app.
  1. Open this page in Chrome, Edge, or Samsung Internet.
  2. Tap the menu (the three dots ⋮ in the top-right).
  3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.
  4. Confirm. The Wanderkind seal lands on your home screen. Open it from there.
