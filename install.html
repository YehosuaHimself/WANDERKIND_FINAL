— Install in two minutes

Two taps and
you're in.

  1. 01

    Open in Safari

    Wanderkind installs as a PWA, and only Safari can do that on iOS. If you're already here in Safari, you're set — skip to step 02.

  2. 02

    Tap the share icon

    The square with the arrow pointing up, at the bottom of Safari. (On iPad, the top.)

  3. 03

    Add to Home Screen

    Scroll past the chat options. Choose Add to Home Screen. Confirm Wanderkind.

    — Then on your home screen:

    Open it from there, not from this browser tab. The home-screen icon launches the real app.

  1. 01

    Open in Chrome

    Or Edge, Samsung Internet — any modern Chromium-based browser supports PWA install.

  2. 02

    Open the menu

    Tap the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner of the address bar.

  3. 03

    Install app

    Tap Install app (or Add to Home screen on older versions). Confirm. The Wanderkind seal lands on your home screen — open it from there.
