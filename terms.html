Terms of Service

By using Wanderkind you agree to the rules below. They're written in plain language because that's the kind of thing Wanderkind is.

Who can use Wanderkind

Anyone 16 or older. Under 18 requires parental consent — you'll be asked at signup. Wanderkind is for people who travel on foot, who open their doors to walkers, or who want to.

What you promise

  • The information you put on your pass is honest.
  • If you host, you treat your guests with dignity.
  • If you walk, you treat your hosts with dignity.
  • You don't use Wanderkind for commercial lodging (that's what other platforms are for).

What we promise

  • Wanderkind is free. There is no premium tier, no fees, no ads.
  • We never sell your data. (See Privacy Policy.)
  • We host your data on infrastructure we trust and we'll tell you if that changes.
  • You can delete your account at any time.

The doctrine

Wanderkind is built on a simple idea: a stranger who walks is no stranger. We open our doors. We carry each other forward. The pass is the credential, the map is the door, the stamps are the story.

Limits

Wanderkind is provided as-is. We don't guarantee uptime, we don't vouch for the safety of any individual host or guest, and we can't be responsible for what happens off-platform. Use your judgment. Trust slowly.
