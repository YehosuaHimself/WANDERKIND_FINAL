— 01 · Brand DNA

Why · How · What

The Golden Circle is the spine. Every design decision must serve one of these three layers.

LayerStatement
WhyWalk free forever.
HowA trusting community of like-minded wanderers who love each other like oneself, in recognition that every Wanderkind is also a host.
WhatGeo-based, convenient host-matching with a credential-centered sovereign travel ID.
MantraEvery Way begins at your door.

— 08 · Navigation

Three Tabs. Map is Home.

The bottom bar holds three buttons. MAP opens on app launch. ME is the personal profile only. MORE holds the six numbered rooms.

PositionLabelRole
LeftMAPHome · find a roof · also surfaces Ways layer

— 09 · Ways

Single-player + Multiplayer

Ways live as a layer inside MAP. Two coexisting modes:

ModeStatusDescription
MultiplayerDefaultOpen the map, find a host, walk. Anywhere. The community is the journey. "Every way begins at your door."
KönigswegFlagshipThe curated single-player Way. Pilot of the medium. Schnitzeljagd elements eventually.
Other WaysDiscoveryCamino Francés · Via Francigena · Jakobsweg · etc. Catalogued for inspiration, walkable single-player.
