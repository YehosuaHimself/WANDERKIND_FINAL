# Wanderkind — Doctrine

> **Every Way begins at your door.**

## The Golden Circle

**Why**
Walk free forever.

**How**
A trusting community of like-minded wanderers who love each other like oneself, in recognition that *every Wanderkind is also a host*.

**What**
Geo-based, convenient host-matching with a credential-centered sovereign travel ID.

## The mantra in one line

**Every Way begins at your door.**

It means: the road is not a fixed route. It is what unfolds when you step out from where you live, in any direction, with the knowledge that someone, somewhere, will welcome you — because somewhere on a different door of the same network, you will welcome someone in return.

## Single-player + multiplayer

Wanderkind has two co-existing modes, by design:

**Multiplayer (default).** Open the map, find a host, walk to them, stay, leave a stamp, continue. Anywhere. Any country. Any road. The community *is* the journey. This is "Every Way begins at your door."

**Single-player (curated, day one essential).** When the community is still being seeded, Wanderkind also ships curated *Ways* — beautifully designed paths a wanderkind can follow without depending on community density.
- **The Königsweg** is the flagship single-player Way. Curated end-to-end. The pilot Way that proves the medium. Eventually, Schnitzeljagd elements (clues, riddles, hidden waypoints) make walking it an unfolding game without crossing the line into gamification.
- **Other Ways** (Camino Francés, Via Francigena, Jakobsweg variants, etc.) are catalogued for discovery and inspiration.

Ways live as a layer inside MAP. The default map view shows hosts; tap the WAYS layer to surface curated routes.

## Performance is doctrine

A PPWA is defined by what it delivers, not what it claims. The numbers we hold to:

- **Lighthouse 100 / 100 / 100 / 100 / 100** (performance · accessibility · best practices · SEO · PWA)
- **First Contentful Paint < 800 ms** on slow 4G
- **Largest Contentful Paint < 1.8 s** on slow 4G
- **Time to Interactive < 2.0 s**
- **Cumulative Layout Shift = 0** (zero, not 0.1)
- **First Input Delay / INP < 100 ms**
- **Bundle ceiling: 250 KB initial paint · 500 KB total app weight**

If a feature would push any of these into the red, the feature gets cut or fixed before merging. No exceptions for "we'll come back to it." This is the perimeter the CI enforces.

## The audience we serve

Wanderkind is for *everyone who moves through the world with intention*. Not pilgrims, not hikers, not backpackers — *Wanderkinder*. Plural: *Wanderkinder*.

The audience runs from 16 to 80+, across every digital-literacy level, across every culture and language. We design for the edges — Rosa (75, walking the Camino with her paper guidebook as backup) and Kai (23, digital nomad) — and the center follows.
