— The doctrine

Every
Wanderkind

Wanderkind is not a service. It is a road, and the road has houses. Every traveler is also someone's host, and every host was once a traveler. This symmetry is what the community lives on.

— Verification

You need no document.
Only your face.

A six-second face scan binds you to your Wanderkind ID. No passport, no driver's licence — just look at the camera. The image never leaves your phone; only a hash is sent.

This is what lets hosts open their doors to you without seeing a piece of paper. The face is the document.
