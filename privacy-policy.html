Privacy Policy

Wanderkind is a community PWA for walkers. We collect the minimum information needed to let wanderkinds find each other and host each other. We never sell your data.

What we collect

  • Account. Email address (for sign-in via OTP code) and a chosen trail name. Your civilian name is never stored after first sign-in.
  • Pass. Optional bio, avatar, home country, host details (when you open your door).
  • Location. Only when you explicitly mark yourself as walking or hosting. Coarse location only — city-level. You can hide your location at any time.
  • Stamps and vouches. Records of nights you walked or hosted, written by hosts. These appear on your public pass.

What we don't collect

  • No tracking pixels, no analytics cookies, no third-party scripts.
  • No precise GPS coordinates without your explicit consent.
  • No payment information — Wanderkind is free forever.
  • No ads. Ever.

Who can see your data

  • Your public pass (trail name, bio, stamps, tier) is visible to other wanderkinds via the map and by URL.
  • Your email is never shown publicly.
  • You can mark your profile private at any time — it disappears from the map and search.

How long we keep it

For as long as your account exists. Delete your account in Settings to remove your profile, stamps, and all linked data. Email us at hello@wanderkind.love if you need help.

Updates

If this policy changes, we'll notify you in the app before the change takes effect. Last updated MMXXVI.
